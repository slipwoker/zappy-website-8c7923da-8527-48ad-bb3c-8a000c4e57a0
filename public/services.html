    Destination Wedding Photography

    Your wedding day is one of life's most significant celebrations, and as a destination wedding photographer, I'm honored to travel with you to capture every moment. Whether you're exchanging vows on a tropical beach, in a European castle, or atop a mountain peak, I document your love story with artistic vision and attention to detail.

    My destination wedding photography approach combines photojournalistic storytelling with fine art aesthetics. I capture candid moments of joy, tears, and laughter alongside carefully composed portraits that showcase the beauty of your chosen location. From getting ready to the final dance, I'm there to preserve every precious memory.

    Elopement Photography

    Elopements offer couples the freedom to celebrate their love authentically, without the pressures of traditional weddings. As an experienced elopement photographer, I specialize in documenting intimate ceremonies in stunning natural settings. Whether you're planning a sunrise ceremony in the mountains, a forest wedding, or an urban adventure, I capture the raw emotion and beauty of your special day.

    My elopement photography style emphasizes natural light, genuine moments, and the unique connection between you and your partner. I work with you to scout locations, plan timing for optimal lighting, and ensure your vision comes to life. The result is a collection of images that tell your authentic love story.

    Elopement packages are flexible and customizable, ranging from intimate two-hour sessions to full-day adventures. Services include location scouting assistance, timeline planning, all digital images with print rights, and a private online gallery for sharing with loved ones.

    Newborn Photography

    The first weeks of your baby's life pass quickly, making newborn photography essential for preserving these fleeting moments. As a trusted newborn photographer near me, I create gentle, safe, and beautiful portraits that capture your baby's tiny features and peaceful expressions. My studio is equipped with professional lighting, comfortable props, and everything needed for a relaxed session.

    Safety is my top priority during newborn sessions. I'm trained in newborn posing and handling, ensuring your baby remains comfortable throughout the photography experience. Sessions typically take place within the first two weeks of birth when babies are sleepiest and most flexible for those adorable curled-up poses.

    Newborn photography packages include a consultation to discuss your style preferences, a two to three-hour session allowing time for feeding and soothing, multiple setups with various props and backdrops, and a selection of professionally edited high-resolution images. Family and sibling photos can be incorporated to document your growing family.

    Maternity Photography

    Pregnancy is a transformative journey deserving of beautiful documentation. Maternity photography sessions celebrate the anticipation, beauty, and connection between expectant parents and their growing baby.
